Information about us

We provide advice and support to empower anyone experiencing a mental health problem. We campaign to improve services, raise awareness and promote understanding.

In reality 'we' is just me. just one person. I was inspired by my friend who recently told me that she believed that she had DID.
In all honesty, as much as I wanted this website to be something I can share to her, I think it is a lot better if I gave her links to resources she can use while researchng on my own to save her time.
I hope that with the information I learned from making this website will carry onto my future where I can help others who I encounter who think they have DID.
I enjoyed desiging this website. I wanted something a bit more different than my other websites, which normally looked a bit more linear and had the user scroll through so much information. I also wanted to work on making my website uniform with a theme.
One of the things that was kind of disppointing was how I could not really use the navigation that I had for my previous website. It wouldve worked greatly but there was something that was not displaying right even though I copied exactly what I used before.
